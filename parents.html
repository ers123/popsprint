Parents and Guardians

Last updated: February 7, 2026

Game Design Goals

  • Short rounds with simple one-tap controls.
  • No account creation required.
  • Low battery and low data usage behavior.

Ad Experience Goal

Keep ads low-frequency and non-disruptive. Do not block gameplay flow with high-pressure ad formats for young players.

Safety Setup Before Public Release

  • Enable child-directed ad controls where required.
  • Use non-personalized ad requests for child audiences where needed.
  • Publish clear contact and policy pages before scale launch.
